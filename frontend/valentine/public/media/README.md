# Romantic Media Assets

This directory contains all the media files for your Valentine's Day website. Replace the placeholder content with your personal photos, videos, and audio to make it truly special.

## Directory Structure

```
media/
├── photos/          # Your personal photos with gunnu
├── videos/          # Romantic video memories
├── audio/           # Background music and sound effects
└── animations/      # Lottie animation files (optional)
```

## Photos (photos/)
Add high-quality photos of you and gunnu. Recommended formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
- `memory-1.jpg` - A beautiful photo of you two together
- `memory-2.jpg` - A candid moment
- `memory-3.jpg` - A romantic pose
- `memory-4.jpg` - Something that represents your love story

## Videos (videos/)
Add short romantic video clips. Recommended formats: MP4, WebM
- `romantic-moment.mp4` - A special video memory
- `love-story.mp4` - A compilation of your moments together

## Audio (audio/)
Add romantic background music and sound effects.
- `romantic-background.mp3` - Soft romantic music (instrumental recommended)
- `heart-beat.mp3` - Subtle heart beat sound for interactions
- `sparkle.mp3` - Gentle sparkle sound effect

## Tips for Best Results
- **Photos**: Use high-resolution images (at least 1920x1080)
- **Videos**: Keep them short (30-60 seconds) and optimized for web
- **Audio**: Use royalty-free music or your own recordings
- **File Names**: Use descriptive names without spaces

The website will automatically detect and use your media files!
